BART Ridership in 2017

When you highlight a station, the circles resize and the bands fade to represent the ridership between that station and the others. The buttons decide if we see ridership to or from the highlighted station. Like if you scroll over Embarcadero, and 24th Street gets big, it means a lot of riders go from 24th St. to Embarcadero.
Data from June 2013 (the month before the strike).
